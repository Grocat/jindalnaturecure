What Makes Our
Campus Green?

At Jindal Naturecure Institute, nature is not just a backdrop—it is a core part of therapy. Our campus is home to over 3,438+ trees across 56 clusters, carefully preserved and nurtured by our horticulture team.

From medicinal plants to shaded healing spaces, every element is designed to align with the principles of natural healing. The biodiversity of our campus creates a living, breathing sanctuary for wellness and recovery.

Documenting Our
Green Heritage

Our campus tree census has scientifically documented every major tree species, including their botanical names. Informational signage is being introduced to enhance awareness and engagement for our patients and visitors.

Each tree is cataloged with species identification, growth records, and ecological role—building a living library of our natural heritage.
